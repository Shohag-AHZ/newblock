The UK offers various scholarships and financial aid options for international students. These scholarships are provided by universities, governmental organisations, and private institutions. Here are some popular scholarships available in the UK for international students:

University Scholarships

Many UK universities offer their own scholarships and bursaries for international students. These scholarships can be merit-based, need-based, or specific to certain fields of study. Each university has its own scholarship programmes, so it's important to check individual university websites for details.

This is the most popular route for scholarship, please discuss with your counsellor as to which scholarship you may be eligible for. You can contact us here.

Chevening Scholarships

These prestigious scholarships are funded by the UK government and offer full financial support to outstanding students from around the world to pursue a master's degree at any UK university.

Commonwealth Scholarships and Fellowships

The Commonwealth Scholarship Commission offers scholarships and fellowships to students from Commonwealth countries to pursue master's or doctoral studies in the UK. These scholarships cover tuition fees, living expenses, and travel costs.

British Council Scholarships

The British Council offers several scholarships and funding programmes for international students, including the GREAT Scholarships programme, which provides financial support for students from specific countries to study in the UK.

Subject-Specific Scholarships

Various organizations and foundations offer scholarships for specific fields of study. For example, the Royal Society, Wellcome Trust, and Gates Cambridge Scholarship are prestigious scholarships in the fields of science, health, and research.

These are just a few examples of the scholarships available in the UK for international students. It's important to note that eligibility criteria, application processes, and deadlines vary for each scholarship. It is advisable to thoroughly research and review the specific requirements and application procedures for each scholarship to determine your eligibility and maximise your chances of success.
